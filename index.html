  •       Biography      

    • Dr. Nan Tang is a senior scientist at Qatar Computing Research Institute (QCRI), HBKU, Doha, Qatar. Prior to joining QCRI in Dec 2011, He was a Research Fellow at LFCS (Laboratory for Foundations of Computer Science) at the University of Edinburgh, Edinburgh, UK (2010--2011). He was a scientific staff member with the CWI (Dutch National Research Center for Mathematics and Computer Science), Amsterdam, Netherlands (2008--2010). He got his PhD. degree from The Chinese University of Hong Kong, China (2007). He holds a visiting position at University of Waterloo, Canada (03/2007-08/2007).
