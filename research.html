  •        Network Monitoring       

    • On Summarizing Graph Streams

    Massive graphs arise in many applications e.g., network traffic data and social networks. They are highly dynamic. In addition, real-time analytics over big graph streams is urgently needed. To enable such analytics, we study the problem of summarizing graph streams. Specifically, given a graph stream G, directed or undirected, the objective is to summarize G as S with a much smaller (sublinear) space, linear construction time and constant maintenance cost for each edge update, such that S allows many queries over G to be approximately conducted efficiently.

    Prediction using Graph Sketches

    Beyond monitoring networks using our graph sketches, we also study the problem of predicing the future using our graph sketches.
